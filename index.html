About us

Rita Giordano, a UK-based data visualisation consultant and scientific illustrator, started the visual data and science studio. She worked for several years in research and in the data science industry. By training, she is a physicist who holds a PhD in statistics applied to structural biology. Furthermore, she has more than fourteen years of professional experience in data analysis and statistics. Over the years, she has developed her skills in illustration by attending specifically targeted short courses.
