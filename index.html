About us

Rita Giordano visual data and science studio is founded by Rita Giordano, a UK-based data visualisation designer and scientific illustrator. She worked for several years in research and the data science industry. She is a physicist by training and hold a PhD in statistics applied to structural biology. She has more than thirteen years of experience in data analysis and statistics. During these years, she broaden her skills in illustration, attending specifically targeted short courses. Courses delivered by the University of arts of London (UAL) and “Ecole des beaux arts” of Grenoble, France. She is passionate about data storytelling, illustrating scientific research and providing accessible visualisations. The vision of her studio is to make data and science accessible to not experts.
