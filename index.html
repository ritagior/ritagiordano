About us

The visual data and science studio is founded by Rita Giordano, a UK-based data visualisation designer and scientific illustrator. She worked for several years in research and in the data science industry. She is a physicist by training and holds a PhD in statistics applied to structural biology. She has more than thirteen years of experience in data analysis and statistics. During these years, she broaden her skills in illustration, attending specifically targeted short courses. Courses delivered by the University of arts of London (UAL) and “Ecole des beaux-arts” of Grenoble, France. She is passionate about data storytelling, illustrating scientific research and providing accessible visualisations. The vision of the studio is to make data and science accessible to not experts.
